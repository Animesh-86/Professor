Machine Learning Integrated Materials Design Group

Machine learning integrated Materials Design (MLMD) is a collaborative research group, working to accelerate the development of new materials. We are based at NIT Warangal, in the Department of Mechanical Engineering. Our group uses a combination of machine learning (ML), density functional theory (DFT), and Molecular dynamics (MD) simulation to design new materials through computational methods and to help accelerate the experimental characterization of materials. We collaborate extensively with fellow academics, national research centers, and various industry partners.
